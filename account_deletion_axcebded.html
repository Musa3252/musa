Account Deletion Information — Axcebded

This page, provided by developer Musa Chuma, explains how users can permanently delete their Axcebded account and what happens to their data afterward. Deletion must be done inside the Axcebded mobile app.

Overview

Deleting your Axcebded account permanently removes your profile and most personal data after a short grace period. Some limited records may be retained where legally required. You can find the Delete account option in the app: Profile → Settings → Account → Delete account.

Developer

This app and data handling process are maintained by Musa Chuma, the official developer of the Axcebded application.

What gets deleted

  • Your Axcebded profile, login credentials, and personal settings.
  • Personal content stored only in your account (e.g., drafts, saved data).
  • Connected devices and active sessions are revoked.

What may remain

  • Group messages or shared content visible to others may remain to preserve context.
  • Minimal legal or security records (like logs or invoices) may be retained temporarily.
  • Data in encrypted backups is automatically removed during scheduled purges.

Timeline

  • Request: Start deletion inside the Axcebded app.
  • Grace period: The account is disabled for 7–30 days. Logging in again cancels the deletion.
  • Permanent removal: After the grace period, all eligible data is permanently deleted.

Before you delete

  • Export any data you want to keep.
  • Cancel subscriptions to prevent charges.
  • Make sure you are signed into the correct Axcebded account.

How to delete your account (in the app)

  1. Open the Axcebded app and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Account.
  3. Tap Delete account and follow the prompts.

If you don’t see the delete option, update your app to the latest version.

FAQs

Can I reverse deletion?

Yes — if within the grace period, sign in again to cancel deletion. After final deletion, data cannot be restored.

How long does it take?

Usually 7–30 days after your confirmation. Backup systems may take longer to fully purge encrypted copies.
